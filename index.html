We learn languages discuss issues meet new people

We Talk is a way for you to practice a language through curated discussions on important issues envolving people all over the world.

Expert moderation

All discussions are moderated by someone with certified native-level speaking capabilities for that language. Their role is to help and guide you so that you can get better. The discussion topics are generalist in order to be accessible for everyone.

Community-driven

The discussion topics are just a way of engaging people. The true purpose of this platform is to learn languages through practice, and what better way to do that than with friends? The community is our heart and the reason why people keep coming back.

Go at your own pace

If you don't know what to expect, you're shy or you just don't feel like talking: we got you covered! You can hop in the channel and just listen to the discussions taking place. If you hear something you want to contribute to, just join in!

We Talk

'We Talk' is a platform where people from all over the world can come together and learn from each other. The main goal is to practice a language they already have some grasp on, but in the end many more things can be learned from such enriching interactions. The discussion topics are different in each encounter and are both broad and general. Some examples are environmental issues, globalization, philosophical questions or current events.
