WeTalk lets you practice a language by promoting discussions on everyday issues with people from all over the world.

Expert moderation

All discussions are moderated by someone with certified knowledge in that language. The moderator's role is to help and guide you so that you can get better. The discussion topics are universal in order to be accessible for everyone.

Community-driven

The discussion topics are there to engage people, but the true purpose of this platform is to learn languages through practice, and what better way to do that than with friends? The community is at the heart of WeTalk.

Go at your own pace

If you don't know what to expect, if you're shy or just don't feel like talking: we got you covered! You can hop in the channel and just listen to the discussions taking place. If you hear something you want to contribute to, just join in!

WeTalk

WeTalk is a platform where people from all over the world come together and learn from each other. The main goal is to brush up on a language they already have some grasp on, but the contact with so many diverse people may lead to some more learning on its own. The discussion topics are always different and are broad and universal.
